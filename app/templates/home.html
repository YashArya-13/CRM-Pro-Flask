About CRM Pro

CRM Pro is a Flask-based Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system that allows multiple users with different roles — such as Admin, Manager, Sales, and Accountant — to collaborate securely. It’s built for businesses that need efficiency, transparency, and automation in client management.
